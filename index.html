Seattle's Housing Costs Are Unevenly Distributed

By Alejandra Chavarria

A bubble chart view of 2025 apartment rent patterns across Seattle

How to read the chart

Bubble size represents median apartment rent per unit. Bubble color represents rent category: green for low rent, yellow for medium rent, and red for high rent.

What the bubbles show

Seattle's apartment rent does not rise evenly across the city. Each bubble represents a community reporting area, with larger bubbles showing higher median apartment rent per unit. The large red bubbles show that high-rent areas remain especially visible in places such as Downtown, Queen Anne, Capitol Hill, and other central neighborhoods.
